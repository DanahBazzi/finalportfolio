Drawings

Most of my drawings are done in pencil and oil pastels. I love to draw my pet bunny, but she never sits still for long enough for me to draw her portrait, so almost all of my drawings are done using a reference photo.

In addition to drawing pets, I love to draw scenes that are inspired by nature.
